About

I’ve been a product designer long before I pursued it as a career, starting with illustrating coloring books for student orgs and designing yearbook pages as a teen.

(I took a detour into finance and consulting before routing back on the design path. This included getting a degree in accounting with a minor in data analytics at USC, working at a Big 4 firm, and being exposed to SaaS from a financial standpoint.)

While I enjoyed optimizing spreadsheets for executives, I found a stronger passion in helping people by crafting meaningful digital experiences. So far, those have mostly been in the form of mobile app design, but I’m constantly adding to my repertoire.

(For example, I built this site with the help of agentic AI.)

These days, when I’m not designing, I'm either doing yoga, caring for my rabbit Willow, humbling myself practicing wheel throwing, or just being out in the sun.

Thank you for stopping by. If you'd like to chat, please connect with me.
